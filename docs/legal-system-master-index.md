## Section Descriptions

### 01_Public_Website
All guest-facing legal documents published on the website. Must be aligned with operational reality and reviewed before any public claims go live.

### 02_Guest_Documents
Documents provided to or governing guest stays. Includes pre-arrival communications, incident handling, and refund decisions.

### 03_Owner_and_Property_Management
Agreements and terms governing property owner relationships. Core commercial documents for the business model.

### 04_Suppliers_and_Contractors
Third-party service provider agreements. Covers cleaning, maintenance, photography, software, and other vendors.

### 05_Employment_and_Internal
Internal governance documents. Staff agreements, delegation frameworks, and operational policies.

### 06_Disputes_and_Enforcement
Templates and procedures for handling conflicts. Evidence preservation, formal notices, and escalation paths.

### 07_Clause_Library
Reusable legal clauses for drafting. Standardised language for common contractual situations.

### 08_Registers_and_Controls
Tracking and monitoring documents. Live registers for contracts, disputes, claims, and review schedules.
